# Bundled Milkdrop presets

A curated subset (~325, stride-sampled for author/style diversity) of the
projectM **Cream of the Crop** pack:
https://github.com/projectM-visualizer/presets-cream-of-the-crop

Companion textures (in `../textures/`) are from the projectM
**Milkdrop texture pack**:
https://github.com/projectM-visualizer/presets-milkdrop-texture-pack

Licence (per the upstream LICENSE.md): Milkdrop presets were, in almost all
cases, not released under any specific licence; they were freely released and
are treated as public domain. Textures likewise.

We write no presets — these are borrowed, per DESIGN.md.
